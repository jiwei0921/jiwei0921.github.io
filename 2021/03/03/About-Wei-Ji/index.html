About me

I am currently a first-year PhD student in the department of ECE, University of Alberta, Canada and work with Prof. Li Cheng in the VLLab. Prior to joining UAlberta, I graduated from the department of software engineering, Dalian University of Technology and I was mentored by Prof. Miao Zhang and Yongri Piao, under the supervision of Prof. Huchuan Lu. My research interests include segmentation, visual attention, medical image analysis and mutli-modal fusion learning.
